Mantlz's email campaign feature lets you send targeted messages to users who submitted specific forms. Build newsletters, announcements, follow-ups, and more with an intuitive campaign builder.

<Note>
Email campaigns are available on Standard and Pro plans. Free plan users can preview the interface but need to upgrade to send campaigns.
</Note>

## Accessing Campaigns

Navigate to campaigns from your dashboard:

1. Click "Campaigns" in the main navigation menu
2. View all campaigns associated with your forms
3. Filter by form or campaign status

## Campaign List

The campaigns page displays all your email campaigns in a table or grid view:

### Campaign Table Columns

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | Campaign title and description |
| **Form** | Associated form whose subscribers will receive the email |
| **Status** | Draft, Scheduled, Sent, or Failed |
| **Recipients** | Number of subscribers who will receive the campaign |
| **Sent** | Date and time the campaign was sent |
| **Opens** | How many recipients opened the email |
| **Clicks** | Click-through rate on links |

## Creating a Campaign

### Prerequisites

- At least one form with submissions
- Active Standard or Pro subscription
- Verified sender email address

### Campaign Creation Dialog

Click "Create Campaign" to open the composer:

#### Campaign Details

**Campaign Name** (Required)
- Internal identifier for your campaign
- Not visible to recipients
- Example: "Welcome Series #1", "Product Launch Announcement"

**Description** (Optional)
- Internal notes about the campaign
- Helps organize multiple campaigns
- Useful for team collaboration

#### Email Content

**Subject Line** (Required)
- The email subject recipients will see
- Keep it clear and compelling
- Best practice: 40-50 characters

<Tip>
Use action-oriented subject lines that create urgency or curiosity. "Your feedback was received - here's what's next" performs better than "Thank you".
</Tip>

**Email Body** (Required)
- The main content of your email
- Supports HTML formatting
- Minimum 200 characters recommended

### HTML Support

Format your email with HTML:

```html
<p>Welcome to our community!</p>

<p>Thank you for <strong>signing up</strong> for our waitlist.</p>

<p>We'll notify you when we launch. In the meantime:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Follow us on <a href="https://twitter.com/yourcompany">Twitter</a></li>
  <li>Read our <a href="https://blog.example.com">blog</a></li>
</ul>

<p><em>The Team</em></p>
```

Supported tags:
- `<p>` - Paragraphs
- `<strong>`, `<b>` - Bold text
- `<em>`, `<i>` - Italic text
- `<a href="">` - Links
- `<ul>`, `<ol>`, `<li>` - Lists
- `<br>` - Line breaks
- `<h1>` through `<h6>` - Headings

### Draft Management

The campaign composer includes auto-save:

- **Auto-save** - Saves every 30 seconds while editing
- **Manual save** - Click the save icon to save immediately
- **Last saved** - Timestamp showing when draft was last saved
- **Clear draft** - Reset button to start over
- **Draft restoration** - Automatically loads saved draft when reopening

<Info>
Drafts are stored in your browser's local storage and persist even if you navigate away or close the tab.
</Info>

## Preview Mode

Before sending, preview your email:

1. Click the preview icon in the composer header
2. See exactly how recipients will see your email
3. Review subject line and content formatting
4. Check links and styling

Preview displays:
- Subject line as email header
- Sender information ("From: Your Campaign")
- Recipient placeholder ("To: Form Subscribers")
- Rendered HTML content

## Sending Campaigns

### Send Now

Send immediately to all form subscribers:

1. Complete all required fields
2. Review content in preview mode
3. Click "Create" button
4. Confirm send action
5. Campaign status changes to "Sending"

### Schedule for Later

Set a future send time:

1. Click "Schedule" instead of "Send"
2. Select date and time
3. Choose timezone
4. Confirm scheduling
5. Campaign status shows "Scheduled" with send time

#### Scheduled Campaign Management

For scheduled campaigns:

- View send date in the calendar icon badge
- See countdown: "X days until sending"
- Edit before send time
- Cancel scheduling if needed

<Tip>
Schedule campaigns for optimal engagement times. Analytics show when your users are most active.
</Tip>

### Campaign Header

- Campaign name and description

  </Card>

### Detailed Metrics

**Open Rate**
- Percentage of recipients who opened the email
- Tracked via invisible pixel
- Industry average: 20-25%

**Click Rate**
- Percentage who clicked at least one link
- Measures engagement and interest
- Industry average: 2-5%

**Bounce Rate**
- Emails that couldn't be delivered
- Hard bounces (invalid addresses)
- Soft bounces (temporary issues)

**Unsubscribe Rate**
- Recipients who opted out
- Shows at bottom of campaign metrics
- Important for list health

### Performance Chart

Visualize campaign performance over time:

- **Opens over time** - When recipients opened emails
- **Clicks over time** - Engagement timeline
- **Geographic distribution** - Where opens occurred
- **Device breakdown** - Desktop vs mobile opens

### No Analytics Message

For recently sent campaigns:

- "Analytics data is still being collected"
- Check back in a few hours
- Initial data appears within 1-2 hours

## Campaign Actions

Manage existing campaigns:

### Edit Campaign

- Available only for draft campaigns
- Scheduled campaigns can be edited before send time
- Sent campaigns cannot be edited (duplicate instead)

### Duplicate Campaign

1. Click duplicate action
2. Creates new draft with same content
3. Modify as needed
4. Send to same or different form subscribers

### Test Email

Send a test before the actual campaign:

1. Click "Send Test" button
2. Enter test email address
3. Receive preview email
4. Verify formatting and links

### Delete Campaign

- Remove campaign from list
- Cannot delete sent campaigns (archive instead)
- Confirmation dialog prevents accidents

## Campaign Limits

Plan-based sending limits:

- **Free** - 0 campaigns per month (view only)
- **Standard** - 10 campaigns per month
- **Pro** - Unlimited campaigns

<Note>
If you reach your monthly limit, you'll see an upgrade prompt. Limits reset on the first of each month.
</Note>

## Email Deliverability

### Sender Verification

To ensure high deliverability:

- Campaigns sent from verified Mantlz domain
- SPF and DKIM records configured
- DMARC compliance
- Reply-to set to your email

Mantlz automatically:

- Tracks bounced emails
- Marks invalid addresses
- Excludes bounced addresses from future campaigns
- Provides bounce reports
