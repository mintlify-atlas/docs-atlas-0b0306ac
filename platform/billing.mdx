The billing dashboard provides complete control over your Mantlz subscription. Manage payment methods, view invoice history, and upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time.

## Accessing Billing

Navigate to billing from your dashboard:

1. Click "Billing" in the main navigation menu
2. View current subscription status
3. Access invoice history
4. Manage payment methods

## Upgrading Your Plan

Upgrade at any time with immediate access:

### Upgrade Process

1. Click "Upgrade Plan" button
2. Select your desired tier
3. Choose billing frequency (monthly/annual)
4. Enter payment information
5. Confirm upgrade

### Prorated Billing

When upgrading mid-cycle:

- Credit applied for unused time on current plan
- Charged prorated amount for new plan
- New billing cycle starts immediately
- Access to new features is instant

**Example:**

If you upgrade from Free to Standard 15 days into your billing period:

- Receive credit for remaining 15 days of Free plan
- Pay prorated Standard plan cost for 15 days
- Next full billing on the original renewal date

## Downgrading Your Plan

Downgrade to a lower tier:

### Downgrade Process

1. Navigate to subscription settings
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select lower tier
4. Review feature changes
5. Confirm downgrade

### Downgrade Timing

- Takes effect at the end of current billing period
- Retain current plan features until renewal
- No immediate charge or refund
- Data retention adjusts to new plan limits

<Note>
When downgrading, ensure your usage fits within the new plan's limits. Excess forms or data may need to be removed before the downgrade processes.
</Note>

## Invoice History

View and download all past invoices:

### Invoice List

The billing history section displays:

- **Invoice number** - Unique identifier for each invoice
- **Date** - When the invoice was generated
- **Amount** - Total charged (in your local currency)
- **Status** - Paid, Pending, or Failed
- **Download** - PDF download button
- **View online** - Link to hosted invoice page

### Invoice Details

Each invoice includes:

- Billing period covered
- Plan type and pricing
- Prorated adjustments (if applicable)
- Taxes and fees
- Payment method used
- Transaction ID

### Downloading Invoices

1. Locate the invoice in your history
2. Click "Download PDF" button
3. Invoice downloads immediately
4. File named: `mantlz-invoice-{number}.pdf`

<Tip>
Need invoices for accounting? Download all invoices at once using the "Export All" button at the top of the billing history section.
</Tip>

### Empty Invoice History

If you're on the Free plan:

- "No invoices found" message appears
- Explanation that Free plan has no billing
- Upgrade prompt to start paid subscription

### Adding a Payment Method

1. Click "Add Payment Method"
2. Enter card details securely (processed by Stripe)
3. Set as default payment method
4. Verify with small test charge (refunded immediately)

### Updating Payment Method

1. Navigate to payment methods section
2. Click "Update" on existing method
3. Enter new card information
4. Save changes
5. Previous method removed automatically

### Security

All payment processing is handled by Stripe:

- PCI DSS compliant
- End-to-end encryption
- No card details stored on Mantlz servers
- 3D Secure authentication supported
- Fraud detection and prevention

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails:

### Automatic Retry

- First retry: 3 days after failure
- Second retry: 5 days after failure
- Third retry: 7 days after failure
- Account suspension: 10 days after failure

### Notifications

You'll receive emails:

- Immediately when payment fails
- Before each retry attempt
- Warning before account suspension
- Confirmation when payment succeeds

### Updating Failed Payment

1. Log in to your dashboard
2. Banner notification appears
3. Click "Update Payment Method"
4. Enter new card details
5. Click "Retry Payment Now"

<Note>
If your account is suspended due to failed payment, you'll lose access to premium features but your data remains safe. Update your payment method to restore access immediately.
</Note>

## Canceling Your Subscription

Cancel at any time with no penalties:

### Cancellation Process

1. Navigate to billing settings
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Select cancellation reason (optional feedback)
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Receive confirmation email

### After Cancellation

- Retain paid plan access until end of billing period
- Automatically downgrade to Free plan on renewal date
- All forms and data remain accessible
- Premium features become unavailable after period ends
- Can reactivate subscription anytime

### Data Retention After Cancellation

- **Forms** - All forms remain active
- **Submissions** - Full history retained for 90 days
- **Analytics** - Visible for 30 days
- **Campaigns** - History saved but no new sends
- **Files** - Uploaded files retained for 60 days

## Tax Information

Manage tax details for invoices:

### Adding Tax ID

1. Navigate to billing settings
2. Click "Add Tax Information"
3. Enter VAT/GST number
4. Select country/region
5. Save tax details

### Automatic Tax Calculation

Mantlz automatically:

- Calculates applicable sales tax
- Applies VAT for EU customers
- Handles GST for applicable regions
- Updates tax rates when regulations change

## Billing Contact

Update who receives billing notifications:

1. Navigate to billing settings
2. Click "Billing Contact"
3. Enter email address for invoices
4. Optionally add company name and address
5. Save changes

Billing emails sent to this address:
- Monthly/annual invoices
- Payment confirmations
- Failed payment alerts
- Subscription change confirmations
