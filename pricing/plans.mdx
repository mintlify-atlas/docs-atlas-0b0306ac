## Upgrading & Downgrading

### How to Upgrade
1. Navigate to your dashboard settings
2. Click on "Upgrade Plan"
3. Select your desired plan
4. Enter payment information
5. Confirm upgrade

**Immediate Benefits:**
- Increased quotas take effect immediately
- Pro-rated billing for the current month
- Access to advanced features right away

### How to Downgrade
1. Navigate to your dashboard settings
2. Click on "Manage Subscription"
3. Select "Change Plan"
4. Choose your new plan
5. Confirm downgrade

**Important Notes:**
- Downgrade takes effect at the next billing cycle
- Current features remain available until cycle ends
- Excess forms will need to be deleted before downgrade completes
- Historical data is always preserved

<Note>
  If you have more forms than your new plan allows, you'll be prompted to delete forms before the downgrade can complete.
</Note>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I downgrade?">
    All your historical data (forms, submissions, analytics) is preserved regardless of plan changes. However, if you have more active forms than your new plan allows, you'll need to delete some forms before the downgrade completes.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I purchase additional submissions without upgrading?">
    Currently, we don't offer add-on submission packs. If you consistently exceed your plan's limits, we recommend upgrading to the next tier for better value.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer annual billing discounts?">
    We currently offer monthly billing only. Annual billing with discounts may be available in the future. Enterprise customers can contact sales for custom billing arrangements.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What counts as a submission?">
    A submission is counted each time a user successfully submits a form. Partial submissions, validation errors, and spam are not counted toward your quota.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I use campaigns on the Starter plan?">
    No, email campaigns are only available on Standard ($8/month) and Professional ($15/month) plans. The Starter plan focuses on form collection and basic notifications.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
