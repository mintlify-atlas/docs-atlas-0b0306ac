## What's Included

All Mantlz plans include these core features:

- **Form Builder** - Powerful schema-based form creation with validation
- **Real-time Analytics** - Track form performance and submission trends
- **Email Notifications** - Automated confirmation and notification emails
- **API Access** - Full API access for custom integrations
- **Webhook Support** - Real-time data delivery to your applications
- **Custom Domains** - Connect your own domain for branded forms
- **Export Data** - Download submissions in CSV or JSON format

<Note>
  All plans are billed monthly in USD. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime without penalties.
</Note>

## Billing & Payment

### Monthly Billing Cycle
- Plans are billed on the first of each month
- Quotas reset on the first day of each month at 00:00 UTC
- Pro-rated billing when upgrading mid-cycle
- No credit card required for the Starter plan

### Accepted Payment Methods
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- Processed securely through Stripe

  Need custom limits or enterprise features? [Contact our sales team](mailto:sales@mantlz.com) for custom pricing.

## Quota System

Mantlz uses a monthly quota system to manage resource usage across all plans:

### What Resets Monthly
- **Submission count** - Resets to 0 on the first of each month
- **Campaign count** - Resets for Standard and Professional plans
- **Email metrics** - Opens, clicks, and sent counts reset

### What Carries Over
- **Forms** - Your created forms remain active
- **Historical data** - All past submissions and analytics are preserved
- **Settings** - All configurations and customizations persist

<Note>
  You'll receive a warning email 3 days before month-end if you've used more than 10% of your quota, giving you time to export data or upgrade.
</Note>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change plans at any time?">
    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged a pro-rated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my submission limit?">
    If you reach your monthly submission limit, new submissions will be rejected until your quota resets on the first of the next month. You can upgrade your plan at any time to increase your limits immediately.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do unused submissions roll over to the next month?">
    No, submission quotas reset on the first day of each month. However, all historical submission data is retained permanently regardless of your current plan.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial for paid plans?">
    The Starter plan is free forever and doesn't require a credit card. You can test all core features before upgrading to a paid plan. We don't offer separate trials since you can start free and upgrade when needed.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I cancel my subscription?">
    Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period, then automatically downgrade to the free Starter plan.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
